# Week 02 — notes

What I learned this week:

* Removing substrings with REPLACE()
* Joining tables as lookups
* Combining several columns into one with ||
* A good use case for CTEs
* Pivoting and unpivoting with UNPIVOT
* More date functions: TO_DATE with a format mask, QUARTER
* Stacking tables with UNION ALL (SQL's version of a wildcard union)
* More CTE usage, including chaining them
* PIVOT to spread rows into columns
* ROW_NUMBER with PARTITION BY and QUALIFY to remove duplicates
* DATE_FROM_PARTS to build a date from numbers
